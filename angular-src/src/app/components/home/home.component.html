About

Half & Half is bringing the convenience of automation to the grocery industry.

We aim to simplify grocery shopping without eliminating the retail experience.
In order to use Half & Half, simply click on the store and start selecting items you'd like to purchase
When you're done, finish by clicking place order
Schedule a time to pick up the order and provide your email address plus credit information
We'll send you an email with your order number
When your scheduled time arrives, simply go to the store to pick up your itmes
Once there, you can begin selecting items from our selfserve section
When you finish up, just go to the checkout, give your order number
We'll ring you up and hand you the items you picked out online
Since you provided your credit card information earlier, you don't have to worry about having to pay
