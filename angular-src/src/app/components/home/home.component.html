About

Bot Stop is an automated grocery store bringing the convenience
of automation while maintaining the core shopping experience.

How it works

Simply use our online store to create an order. Once your order
is filled out, you can schedule a pick up time or simply pop by
in the next 20-30 minutes to retrieve your order at the store.
Once at the store you can grab any additional items you'd like
and simply bring your items to the checkout where they'll have
your order ready made for you to pick up.
